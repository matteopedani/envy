LAIKAILIKE, THE FIRST LUXURY HANDBAG AS YOU LIKE

LaikaIlike comes from an idea of Davide Lamperti.

In a world where the technology is constantly conditioning our lives and the selling online is increasing day by day, Laikailike is the perfect combination between the renowned Italian quality and the unique personalization granted to the customer.

With a past as space modelus designer, Davide decides to apply his experience to the company that his parents Clara and Sandro founded over 40 years ago, and today the first three-dimensional configurator for purses has finally become reality. Any client is now allowed to select the desired bag and customize it in every single detail, choosing among a great number of leathers, linings and zips.

Thanks to the advanced technology used for this configurator the customer can see in real time his own bag in a computerized reproduction extremely accurate and faithful to reality.

All LaikaILike bags are produced inside our company located in Varese www.valigeria-alex.com.

High quality leathers, 360 degrees of personalization and quality of Made in Italy make LaikaILike a unique product.

LaikaILike, the first luxury handbag as you like.
